Tate McRae Think Later Reviews

Tate McRae is my favorite singer and songwriter. I'm listing some songs from my favorite album called "think later" and what they mean.

stay done

stay done is about the struggle of trying to stay away from someone you know isn’t good for you, even though the cycle keeps repeating.

calgary

calgary is about a homesick moment tied to her hometown and processing how far she’s come and how disconnected she sometimes feels from her past.

guilty conscience

in an interview, she states if loving someone is the worst thing in the world, then she guesses she'll have to live with a guilty conscience in fear of hurting someone she cares about.

want that too

want that too is about wanting more from someone who won’t give it, and the frustration of feeling like you’re always asking for or giving too much.

messier

messier is a confession about being complicated, emotional, and imperfect and owning the chaos you bring into relationships.

cut my hair

cut my hair is song about letting go of old versions of yourself and trying to regain control when life feels overwhelming.

run for the hills

run for the hills is about a push‑pull dynamic with someone who feels emotionally risky and still wanting them while knowing you should run the other way.

grave

grave is about feeling trapped or mourning something that’s over but still haunting.

plastic palm trees

plastic palm trees is about artificiality, fake people, fake feelings, or the emptiness behind a glamorous life that looks perfect from the outside.

we're not alike

we're not alike is a confrontation song and realizing you and a friend are different in values or behavior caused by deception.

think later

think later is the album’s thesis: impulsive choices, chaotic nights, and living in the moment without worrying about consequences. Tate McRae stated this track is about a real “insane” night in Boston step‑by‑step.

hurt my feelings

hurt my feelings is about vulnerability and wanting someone to challenge you emotionally, even if it means getting hurt, because it makes you feel something real.

exes

exes is about past relationships resurfacing and the messy and petty energy that comes with it.

greedy

greedy is a confident, flirtatious song about owning your desirability and not apologizing for high standards or bold energy.
