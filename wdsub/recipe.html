Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  2. 2 tsp vanilla extract
  3. 1 1/2 tsp vegetable or canola oil
  4. 1 cup plain / all purpose flour
  5. 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
  6. 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
  7. 1/2 cup milk
  8. 1/8 tsp salt
  9. 2 large eggs
  10. 1 cup sugar
  11. frosting or vanilla buttercream

Baking Tools

  1. Cupcake Pan
  2. Cupcake Liners
  3. Mixing Bowls
  4. Electric Mixer Or Whisk
  5. Measuring Cups
  6. Measuring Spoons
  7. Spatula

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) at least 20 minutes before starting
  2. Line a standard muffin tin with cupcake liners

    3. Instructions

    1. Whisk flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl then set it aside
    2. Place butter and milk in a heatproof jug and microwave on high for 2 minutes until the butter is melted then cover it and keep it in the microwave
    3. In a separate bowl, beat eggs for 30 seconds on speed 6 on electric mixer
    4. With the beater still going, pour the sugar in over 30 seconds
    5. In a separate bowl, beat eggs on speed 6 for 30 seconds
    6. Scatter 1/3 of the flour mixture over the surface and mix on speed 1 for 5 seconds
    7. Add half of the remaining flour and mix on speed 1 for 5 seconds
    8. Add the rest of the flour and mix on speed 1 for 5–10 seconds
    9. Pour hot milk, vanilla and oil into the now empty flour bowl
    10. Add about 3/4 cups of the egg batter into the hot milk, then use a whisk to mix until smooth
    11. Pour the milk mixture into the egg batter over 15 seconds, then turn the beater off
    12. Scrape down sides and base of bowl. Beat on Speed 1 for 10 seconds
    13. Pour batter into muffin tin, filling 2/3 of the way up
    14. Pour hot milk, vanilla and oil into the now empty flour bowl
    15. Add about 3/4 cups of the egg batter into the hot milk, then whisk until smooth
    16. Fill muffin tin and fill cupcake liners with batter
    17. Bake for 22 minutes until golden
    18. Rest for 2 minutes to let them cool slightly
    19. Remove them from tin after 2 minutes
    20. Add frosting
